About


Greetings!

I'm a Korean student enrolled in the University of California Irvine. I major computer science and am somewhat halfway through my degree. I would be an upcoming third year at UCI this fall, but am currently back home in Suwon to prepare for entry to the Korean military service starting in November. In the meantime I'm spending time on projects, chilling with friends, and working out.

As my field of study shows, my career is directed towards software and development. I've linked my project hubs and achievements around this website, so be sure to check those out. I currently work at a local startup as an intern, where I plan to stay for the remainder of my time until my service begins. There's lots of learning to do, and although the path seems quite daunting, I'm excited about how things will turn out! I've always wanted to be a part of a startup where innovation and growth happens on a daily basis. I really look forward to where this opportunity will lead me.

I'm also an avid PC gamer. I believe gaming is an art, and a field worth investing time and money into. I love digging around the internet searching for fun indie-titles. I can get really hooked onto a game that I enjoy! A few games that I've recently enjoyed playing are Fez, Crypt of the Necrodancer, Counter Strike, and Hero Seige.

Hi, I'm Chan Woo.


I'm a Korean student majoring in Computer Science at the University of California Irvine. I've recently finished my term of service in the Republic of Korea Army as of August 2018 and have returned to Cali to finish my third year of school.

I was born in Seoul, Korea and grew up in Singapore and Thailand. In 2014 I graduated high school in New Delhi, India. I've spent my life overseas and experienced diversity with a multicultural mindset. Now my family lives in the beautiful city of San Paulo, Brazil. I've paid a visit after my ETS from the army, and my it's gorgeous!

I started studying computer science in my first year of high school. My original intent was to escape myself from being the computer-illiterate person I was amongst my peers... and that path has driven me to a long journey that I never regret taking! I specialize in Artificial Intelligence and I also have plans in game development. My life-goal project would be a challenging hack-n-slash game that incorporates AI behavior accordingly to the player's inputs. There's lots of blueprints in my head to designing projects like this, and I hope my website correctly reflects my ideas and thoughts.

In my free time I usually doodle pixel art on my computer, watch tv shows and streams, take deep naps, play video games and learn martial arts. Checkout my art collection here! I'm an avid PC gamer: I love exploring indie titles on Steam and looking up trivias behind games. Two of my favorites are Counter Strike, and Crypt of the Necrodancer. One game that truely changed my perspective on games is Fez. It's a dimension-exceeding (literally) puzzle platformer with some adorable elements, stunning visuals and a killer soundtrack! I highly recommend this to individuals interested in puzzles and retro art. Sometimes I share my gameplay via Twitch or Youtube, check out my channels too.
